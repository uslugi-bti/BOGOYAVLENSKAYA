Social Media Targeting Methods

Geotargeting

Allows you to customize advertising on Facebook and Instagram depending on the user’s geographic location - country, city, region or even neighborhood.

Targeting by interests

Displays advertisements to users whose lifestyle matches specified interests - for example, shows advertisements for new sneakers to runners.

Time targeting

Allows you to clearly limit the display time of your advertising. This is one of the best targeting methods if you know when your customers are active on social media.

Behavioral targeting

The system analyzes user behavior on social networks and shows your advertising to those who, based on this data, are most likely to make a purchase.

Socio-demographic targeting

Shows your advertisement to users who fall within the specified socio-demographic parameters: gender, age, marital status, social status, etc.

Look-alike

Analyzes the profiles of your current customers and, based on the data obtained, selects similar audiences of users to display advertising.
