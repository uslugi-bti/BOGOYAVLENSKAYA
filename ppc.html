STEPS OF ADVERTISING SETTINGS

Each project undergoes a tailored strategy development, implemented gradually. Launching contextual advertising typically takes 3-5 working days and consists of several stages:

Website Examination

Full immersion into the client's business: analysis of demand, profitability, and seasonality. Development of unique selling propositions and preparation of website improvement recommendations.

Competitor Analysis

Identifying key competitors online. Thoroughly analyzing competitor websites, advantages, advertisements, and campaign settings. Utilizing this information to stand out among others.

Analytics Setup

Setting up Tag Manager and Google Analytics codes. Configuring goals and testing their functionality.

Advertising Launch

Passing ad moderation. Determining the optimal strategy and budget allocation.

Semantic Core Collection

Gathering an extensive list of queries for launching contextual advertising on search engines and networks. Clustering and determining landing pages for each query group.

Account Setup

Preparing ad texts and finding illustrations. Compiling the account structure. Configuring temporary targeting, display regions, UTM tags, sitelinks, extended headlines, and adding negative keywords.

Cost Optimization

Analyzing accumulated statistics and making subsequent adjustments. Expanding the list of negative keywords, adjusting bids based on current contextual advertising results.

Profits of using Contextual Advertising do for your business

Improved Conversion Rates:

Because contextual ads are displayed to users who are already interested in similar products or topics, they are more likely to engage with your ad and take the desired action, resulting in higher conversion rates.

Flexible Targeting Options:

Contextual advertising platforms offer a range of targeting options, allowing you to refine your audience based on factors such as demographics, interests, and location. This flexibility ensures your ads are seen by the most relevant users.

Brand Safety:

Contextual advertising platforms use advanced algorithms to ensure your ads are displayed in brand-safe environments alongside high-quality content. This helps protect your brand reputation and minimizes the risk of appearing next to inappropriate or harmful content.

Targeted Reach:

Contextual advertising allows you to display ads to users based on their interests, online behavior, and the content they are currently consuming, ensuring your message reaches a highly relevant audience.

Increased Visibility:

By placing your ads on relevant websites and alongside related content, contextual advertising increases the visibility of your brand and products, helping you attract more attention from potential customers.

Cost-Effective:

Contextual advertising often operates on a pay-per-click (PPC) model, meaning you only pay when users click on your ad. This cost-effective pricing structure allows you to maximize your advertising budget and achieve a positive return on investment (ROI).

Real-Time Optimization:

Contextual advertising platforms provide real-time data and analytics, allowing you to monitor the performance of your campaigns and make adjustments as needed to improve results. This ability to optimize campaigns on the fly maximizes their effectiveness.
